Experimental Learning

Experiential learning is an educational approach that emphasizes learning through hands-on experiences, reflection, and active engagement. It allows learners to acquire knowledge, develop skills, and gain insights by directly participating in real-world activities, promoting a deeper understanding of the subject matter.

Strong Learning

Strong learning refers to a robust and effective learning process characterized by deep comprehension, retention, and the application of knowledge or skills.
